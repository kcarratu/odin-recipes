Lasagna

Description

This is a perfect lasagna recipe for the holidays

It can be prepared ahead of time and you can even freeze any leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

Steps

  1. Heat water in a dutch over over medium heat
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F

Lasagna

Description

This is a perfect lasagna recipe for the holidays

It can be prepared ahead of time and you can even freeze any leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

Steps

  1. Heat water in a dutch over over medium heat
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F

Sides

Description

This is a great side dish to go with the lasagna

Your guests will love it

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 4 basil leaves
  • 4 slices mozzarella

Steps

  1. Sice tomatoes
  2. Add mozzarella
  3. Top with basil
